About Our Restaurant

Our restaurant brings the rich flavors of Ethiopia to Berlin. With traditional recipes passed down through generations, we offer a unique culinary experience. Our Injera (traditional sourdough flatbread) is made fresh daily and serves as the foundation for our delicious dishes.
